What is Aureon?

Aureon is a comprehensive SaaS platform that automates end-to-end client financial workflows: from contracts and e-signatures to invoicing and payment collection via Stripe.

How do I get started?

Simply sign up for a free account. You can start with our Free plan or try any paid plan with a 14-day free trial. No credit card required.

Is my data secure?

Yes. We use bank-level encryption (AES-256) for data at rest and TLS 1.2+ for data in transit. We're also GDPR-compliant and working towards SOC 2 certification.

Can I cancel anytime?

Yes, you can cancel your subscription at any time. You'll continue to have access until the end of your current billing period.
